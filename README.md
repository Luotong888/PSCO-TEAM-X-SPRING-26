# PSCO-TEAM-X-SPRING-26
# Games Description:

Game 1 : Dice Patterns Challenge
A game in which the player rolls 5 dices and they can choose to keep certain dice while they keep rolling, and a final pattern's scoring is based on:
• Five of a Kind – 50 pts
• Four of a Kind – 40 pts
• Full House (3 matching + 2 matching) – 35 pts
• Straight (1-5 or 2-6) – 30 pts
• Three of a Kind – 25 pts
• Two Pairs – 20 pts
• One Pair – 10 pts
